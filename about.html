Our Story

SpatialVoice was founded in 2025 to help people practice public speaking in a real way. We were very scared of public speaking and even tried to avoid it. Practicing in front of a mirror or with a friend didn’t really help. It felt very different from standing on a stage with lots of people watching, or even talking in a meeting with our managers.

We came up with a solution by using virtual reality, or VR, to make pretend audiences of different sizes with different distractions. This gave people a safe and powerful way to practice public speaking and grow skills and confidence for the real world.
